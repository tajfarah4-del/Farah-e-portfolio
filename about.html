About Me

I’m Farah Tajddine from grade 10B in Jewels International School Of Kinshasa, a student with a deep love for music and creativity. The piano feels like my second language — a place where I can express emotions that words can’t describe. I enjoy learning new melodies, especially the Turkish piece Kara Sevda, which connects me to a beautiful musical style. When I’m not playing piano, I enjoy designing websites, creating art, and working on school projects. This portfolio is a small window into who I am and what inspires me.
